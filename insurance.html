Major players are integrating AI-driven underwriting to mitigate the traditionally high loss ratios associated with electric vehicles, a move that is finally beginning to stabilize margins after years of underwriting losses in the green energy sector.

An expert committee recommended ILS development; post-approval, IFSCA will issue regulations and a consultation paper for feedback, with SPVs managing bonds to maturity.
