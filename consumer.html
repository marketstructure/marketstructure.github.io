These strategic alliances reflect the increasing interconnectedness of Asian consumer markets as companies leverage complementary strengths to access new customer bases and distribution networks.

A new 40% rate would apply exclusively to luxury and sin goods, while health and life insurance premiums may drop from 18% to 5% or zero. The reforms aim to reduce the tax burden on daily essentials, packaged food, and electronics while boosting MSME competitiveness.
