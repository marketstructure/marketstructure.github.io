In parallel, Chinese hedge funds that previously delivered triple‑digit returns from AI-linked equities are beginning to scale back exposure and execute pre‑defined exit triggers, signaling a shift from momentum-chasing towards risk management in the most crowded segments of the regional AI trade.

The vehicle’s ability to support on-chain subscriptions and redemptions—potentially via both fiat and stablecoins—marks a significant step in the region’s experimentation with tokenized fund infrastructure under a mainstream regulatory framework, with implications for future distribution, liquidity management and cross-border access in regulated digital products.
